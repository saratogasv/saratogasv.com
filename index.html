Saratoga Resident Association @ Saratoga Silicon Valley

The purpose of the Association is to preserve and enhance Saratoga's unique characteristics, and to advocate and maximize the interests of the community. The Association is organized and operated exclusively for charitable and educational purposes under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, or the corresponding section of any future federal tax code, to provide educational information to Saratoga residents so as to promote and encourage residents' participation in the city's decision-making process.

梦想的产生往往是与所处的环境、与平日接触到的事件密切相关的。儿时的梦想会在穿梭的时光中如云雾缭绕， 忽隐忽现， 伴随着我们的一生。

当不再为五斗米折腰的时候， 当为家族的繁衍尽到了责任之后， 儿时的梦想就会逐渐清晰起来， 从心底里迸发出要去实现她的冲动。

加州硅谷是个实现梦想的地方。在人杰地灵的硅谷小镇SARATOGA，居住着一群有趣的逐梦人，一群被电脑编程耽误了的木匠、园丁、泥瓦客。 他们愿意与您一起， 畅谈儿时的梦想， 见证努力的过程， 分享成功的喜悦。
